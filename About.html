ℹ️ About CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is a NAAC A+ accredited university located in Changa, Gujarat, India.
Established in 2000, it offers programs in engineering, management,
pharmacy, and applied sciences.


It aims to deliver quality education with a strong focus on
research, innovation, and societal development.
